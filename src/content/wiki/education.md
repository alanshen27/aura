# Education & Communication

## Overview

A major part of our project is making synthetic biology accessible to everyone — not just scientists. We built IDKAASynBio.com as an educational platform to break down complex synbio topics into short, friendly modules.

## The Learning Platform

Our website features:

- **10 beginner modules** covering everything from "What is DNA?" to "Ethics and safety in synthetic biology"
- **3-minute newsletter** — bite-sized synbio lessons delivered to your inbox
- **Interactive quizzes** to test understanding
- **Key terms with analogies** — because "plasmids are like USB drives for bacteria" is way more memorable than a textbook definition

### Why We Built This

Most synbio educational resources are either:

1. University-level textbooks (too dense)
2. Wikipedia articles (too dry)
3. YouTube videos (too long, often overstimulating)

We wanted something in between — short, calm, well-designed modules that anyone can read in 5 minutes.

## Inclusivity

Our educational content is designed with inclusivity in mind:

- Simple language, no jargon without explanation
- Low-overstimulation design (calm colors, no flashy animations)
- Multiple formats (reading, quizzes, newsletters) for different learning preferences
- Free and accessible to anyone with an internet connection
